# Marcy - Merchandiser

**Role**: Merchandiser
**Context**: BO Administration, B2B Storefront
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-5)

## Quote

> "I guarantee the procurement of the appropriate merchandise, at the proper location, within the designated timeframe, in optimal quantities, and at the ideal price."

## Overview

Marcy balances between the expectations of customers, the financial strategy and objectives of the company. To achieve this, Marcy plans and prioritizes stock allocation to stores. It is crucial to select the right amount of merchandise, including styles, brands, colours, sizes, and distribute them to selected stores according to the appropriate season, budget, and location. Her goal is to avoid end-of-season markdowns. She wants to attract customers to generate a reasonable return on investment for the retailer.

## Key Responsibilities

- Run an online business
- Balance customer's expectations & company's strategy
- Define product assortment for basic category management
- Put merchandise in the right place, at the right time
- Plan & prioritize before/after getting stock in stores
- Define right quantities of merchandise at right price
- Understand customer need/demographics to define products

### SaaS
- Optimize inventory levels to reduce stockouts and excess stock
- Make informed decisions based on comprehensive sales and customer data
