# Sam - B2B Buyer

**Role**: B2B Buyer
**Context**: BO Administration, Storefront
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-16)

## Overview

Sam maintains robust industry relationships. He stays fully engaged in delivering exceptional service to vendors and an internal inventory team. Effective time management is crucial for him in order to prioritize tasks, analyze sales, inventory, and coordinate with suppliers. Lastly, it is part of the research and negotiation process.

## Key Responsibilities

- Evaluate hot-selling merchandise to purchase
- Understand customer's needs
- Monitor prices of competitors & build strategy upon
- Reflect 'brand values' in purchasing decisions
- Negotiate with vendors & build relationships
- Support inventory of goods & work within a budget
- Demonstrate flexibility & consistency

### SaaS
- Minimizing disruption to business operations during the transition to SaaS solutions

## Challenges

- Long research & length of purchasing approval process
- Lack of tools to store & update research information
- Absence of option working offline or mobile
- Switching vendors, poor products quality, late delivery
- Poor vendor service
- Overwhelmed by the amount of vendors/suppliers

### SaaS
- Information overload and difficulty comparing different solutions
- Integrating SaaS solutions with existing systems and workflows
- Balancing the need for innovation with the risks associated with new implementations
