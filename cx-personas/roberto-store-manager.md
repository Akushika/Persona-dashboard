# Roberto - Store Manager

**Role**: Store Manager
**Context**: BO Order Fulfillment, Storefront
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-11)

## Quote

> "I am proficient in five key functions: planning, organizing, staffing, leading, and monitoring progress."

## Overview

Roberto is responsible for management of employees and delegating responsibilities to ensure that the store operates smoothly. He develops store strategies that increase the customer base, expand store traffic, and optimize profitability. He supervises the stocking of shelves, ensuring that the store is presentable, safe, and clean for shoppers. In addition, he manages loss prevention efforts with vendors. Lastly, he is responsible for all the financial assets in the register drawers and ensuring a financial well being of the store.

## Key Responsibilities

- Expand store traffic and optimize profitability
- Ensure high levels of customer satisfaction
- Monitor stocks & regulate inventory
- Build personal relationships with regular customers
- Develop store strategies to expand customers' pool

## Challenges

- Arrange staff shifts & absences
- Management/Leadership
- Multi-Store Management
- New Product Launch
- High employee turnover
