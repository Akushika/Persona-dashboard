# Olivia - Omnichannel Operations Manager (SaaS)

**Role**: Omnichannel Operations Manager
**Context**: OMF, OMSA, OMS
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-25)

## Quote

> "Consistency builds trust, no matter where customers shop."

## Overview

Olivia is accountable for growing the Omnichannel business by driving a unified, frictionless experience across all channels. She is responsible for all business functions, customer service, and analysis and product management within the Omnichannel organization. Olivia requires close and collaborative partnerships with executive-level officers across the company as well as managing several external partners to prioritize and enable new customer experiences across multiple digital channels. She is generally characterized by high levels of enterprise-level scope; strategic, functional, and relational complexity, and a high level of independent decision making.

## Key Responsibilities

- Generating innovative solutions to improve operational efficiency
- Translate long-term strategic direction into mid- and short-term strategies
- Increase customer satisfaction by ensuring order promises are consistently met
- Implement a system with consistent inventory and order data for confident decision-making
- Develop a forward-looking system to better manage scalability and extensibility

## Challenges

- Inconsistent customer experience across all sales channels
- Poor inventory visibility resulting to canceled/delayed/unfulfilled orders or backorders
- Challenges in sourcing from the best locations to meet customer demands at minimal business cost
- Lack of feedback on fulfillment activity of an order resulting to an inaccurate status updates
