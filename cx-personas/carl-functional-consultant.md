## Overview

Carl starts his day reviewing project updates and identifying areas where his expertise is needed. He spends his mornings analyzing complex issues, advising teams, and providing recommendations. Midday meetings involve collaborating with stakeholders to align business and technical requirements. In the afternoons, he documents best practices, researches industry trends, and refines project strategies. His day ends with mentoring team members and preparing for upcoming discussions.
