## Overview

The order placement for the product is the first of the many steps I manage. The order fulfillment process requires strategic operations for a high volume of order to fulfill in a multi-shift distribution center. I ensure timely and accurate product receipt and the optimization of resources and processes to operate within budget. Other processes in motion we deal with are order acknowledgement, invoicing, order planning, order changes, processing, shipment, track and trace. Always in compliance with policies & procedures.

## Key Responsibilities

- Ensure compliance with company policies/procedures
- Acknowledge order placement & invoicing
- Manage order updates, shipment, track & trace
- Ensure accurate product receipt
- Strategize & operate a high volume of orders
- Optimize resources & processes
- Manage multi-shift distribution center
