# Natalia - Sales Store Associate

**Role**: Sales Store Associate
**Context**: Storefront features: browse/search personal information, save cart, checkout
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-24)

## Quote

> "I measure a successful day by the satisfaction of our customers."

## Overview

Natalia is responsible for opening the store and conducting an inventory check, as well as managing replenishment and organizing deliveries. She keeps herself up-to-date on the latest products and current promotions. She also, provides personalized recommendations and information on product availability to customers. If a product is not available in store, she offers alternatives and may contact other stores to help customers find what they are looking for. Natalia also handles payments and tracks her sales performance by associating her employee number with transactions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Support customers & assist to select products
- Sell products to customers & write product reviews
- Provide outstanding customer service
- Meet sales goals
- Remain flexible to address any situation or customer need

## Challenges

- Understand price difference (physical & online stores)
- Stock indicated online is not actually in the store
- Customers don't find what they are looking for
- Short-staffed in busy periods & too many orders to fulfill
- Lack of visibility about replenishment
- Onboarding and training for processes & work tools
- Queuing of customers (enter the store, pay or get order)

- Inaccurate or outdated inventory information
