# Brielle - Business Data Analyst

**Role**: Business Data Analyst
**Context**: Analytics
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-21)

## Quote

> "I want to take data to the next level and use every business opportunity for improvement."

## Overview

Brielle specialized on analyzing the store performance data. After selecting different time periods for comparison, she is responsible to deliver trusted as well as reliable data. These insights allows to drive intelligent and strategic business decisions and more so enables business growth. On top of this it facilitates a strong risk discipline. Dependently she updates a large variety of product offerings and she is curious about the different product searches, checkouts, order statuses as well as deeper customer insights.

## Key Responsibilities

- Work efficiently with performance data
- Come up with innovative ideas
- Create & use business opportunities
- Improve data storytelling abilities
- Streamlining data collection by creating routines
- Create personalized reports for the management

### SaaS
- Enable data-driven decision-making by providing actionable insights
- Monitor and enhance business performance through continuous analysis
- Streamline reporting processes to deliver timely and accurate information
- Effectively communicate insights and trends to stakeholders

## Challenges

- Data cannot be exported into correct format
- Bad Visualization
- No customizable options for analytics

- External software needed

### SaaS
- Difficulty tailoring SaaS solutions to fit specific analytical needs
- Reliance on SAP for updates, support, and security
- Potential difficulties in integrating SAP SaaS with non-SAP systems
- Possibly high costs for advanced features or additional user licenses
