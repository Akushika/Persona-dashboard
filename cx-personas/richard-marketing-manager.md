# Richard - Marketing Manager

**Role**: Marketing Manager
**Context**: BO Administration, BO Adaptive Search, ISS
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-4)

## Quote

> "I actively seek out opportunities and explore new channels to market our products."

## Overview

Richard drives sales across multiple product lines. He proactively seeks out opportunities to enhance the product image, monitors market trends, plans advertising campaigns, establishes pricing and targets strategies. As a marketing manager he assesses the needs, habits, and interests of the target audience to launch tailored marketing campaigns based on demographic data. The effectiveness of these campaigns is measured through tracking data and outcomes, enabling necessary adjustments and the pitching of ideas for future campaigns.

## Key Responsibilities

- Sell the most products across product lines
- Monitor market trends & conduct market research
- Create advertising & target marketing campaigns
- Develop pricing & target strategies
- Find opportunities & new channels to market products
- Determine target audience needs, habits & interests
- Manage outstanding campaigns with quantifiable results

### SaaS
- Enhancing customer engagement and satisfaction through targeted communication
- Streamlining marketing processes to improve operational efficiency and productivity
- Making informed marketing decisions based on comprehensive and real-time data
- Maximizing the return on investment (ROI) for marketing campaigns and activities
