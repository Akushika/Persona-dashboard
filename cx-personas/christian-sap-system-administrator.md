# Christian - SAP System Administrator

**Role**: SAP System Administrator
**Context**: Commerce Cloud: Administration, Model T
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-2)

## Quote

> "I am a lead-administrator for Commerce Platforms."

## Overview

Christian is a member of the Customer Operations team and has full administrative access to customers' accounts on Cloud Portal and Automation Engine. He can monitor customer data and environments, perform network operations and configurations, as well as develop, configure, implement, and manage systems. Once the new features are rolled out, he is the one who updates configurations and notifies customers.

## Key Responsibilities

- Monitor customers' data & environments
- Performing network operations & configurations
- Notify when new features are being rolled out
- Create and send notifications to customers
- Administrate customer accounts
- Develop, configure, implement & manage systems

### SaaS
- Quickly adapting to changing business needs through easy-to-configure software
- Reducing IT costs by eliminating the need for hardware and software maintenance
- Facilitating better collaboration and data sharing across the organization
- Taking advantage of regular updates and new features provided by the vendor

## Challenges

- Lack of clarity around the necessity of certain steps
- Hidden or incomplete documentation
- Lack of clarity of setup & successful status
- Understand & resolve customer problems

### SaaS
- Less control over software updates and feature changes
- Ensuring the security and privacy of sensitive data in the cloud
- Difficulty in integrating applications with existing systems
- Dependency on internet connectivity and potential latency issues
