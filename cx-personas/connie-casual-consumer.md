# Connie - Casual Consumer

**Role**: Casual Consumer
**Context**: Storefront
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-8)

## Quote

> "I wish all this stuff was simpler."

## Overview

While Connie used to prefer shopping at local stores, he now also enjoys shopping at trusted online stores that offer secure payment processes and a wide range of products. The search tools are easy to use, and he appreciates the personalized messages on his social media accounts. He noticed that each online store has different services for product search, checkout, and delivery, which can be convenient. Connie still supports local businesses and have started using their curb pick-up and pick-up in-store services as well.

## Key Responsibilities

- Browse and research products & services
- Add goods to cart
- Create wishlists & share with family
- Checkout & pick up in store
- Receive goods at home & write reviews
- Research via social media

## Challenges

- Hidden fees, shipping charges
- Overload of information & options
- Limited trust on how personal data is being handled/payment
- Confusing questions on forms
- Confusing or inconvenient return policy
- Small product pictures or descriptions
- Unfulfilled commitments
