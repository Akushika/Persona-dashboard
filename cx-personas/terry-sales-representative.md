# Terry - Sales Representative

**Role**: Sales Representative
**Context**: BO Administrator, BO Customer Support, BO Order Fulfillment, Composable Store, C4C, SAP CPQ & Commissions
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-28)

## Quote

> "I provide individual negotiation approach for each customer to ensure faster decision making."

## Overview

Terry promotes and sells products/services to existing or prospective customers. It is important to provide clear, updated and sufficient information so that customers are able to accept quotes and complete orders. This is also important to generate leads and negotiate contracts. Establishing, developing and maintaining positive relationships is paramount to exceed sales goals.

## Key Responsibilities

- Use solid evidence to present, promote & sell products/services
- Interact with existing & prospective customers
- Provide clear & updated information for decision-makers
- Prepare or accept quotes & complete orders
- Generate leads & negotiate contracts
- Help with pricing schedules for quotes, promotions
- Establish, develop & maintain relationships

## Challenges

- Missing or scattered information
- Extra clicks during the ordering process
- Finding the best products for up-selling, cross-selling
- Hide element if not needed
- Having to re-enter information which could be pre-filled
- Internal slow quote processing such as waiting from 'legal'
- Holistic view of customer & product insights
- Complex software demonstrations and integrations
- Keeping up with frequent software updates and changes
