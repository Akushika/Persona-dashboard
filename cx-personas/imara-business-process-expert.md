# Imara - Business Process Expert

**Role**: Business Process Expert
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-22)

## Quote

> "Optimized processes lead to efficient operations and better business outcomes."

## Overview

Imara starts her day by reviewing performance reports and identifying bottlenecks in IT workflows. She spends her mornings analyzing data, mapping out process improvements, and consulting with IT teams to align business needs with technical capabilities. Afternoons are filled with meetings, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and refining process models. She ends the day documenting recommendations, ensuring alignment with IT strategy, and planning for the next phase of improvements.

## Key Responsibilities

- Enhance IT process efficiency and effectiveness
- Ensure alignment between business requirements and IT solutions
- Implement sustainable process improvements that drive operational excellence
- Bridge the gap between business needs and IT capabilities
- Establish standardized process frameworks to scale business operations
- Continuous improvement and operational efficiency

## Challenges

- Navigating complex IT processes and identifying areas for optimization to enhance efficiency and effectiveness within the IT LoB
- Balancing the need for process improvements with the constraints of existing IT systems, infrastructure, and resource allocations
- Collaborating with stakeholders across IT departments to address evolving business needs and requirements while maintaining operational stability
