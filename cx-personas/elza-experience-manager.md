# Elza - Experience Manager

**Role**: Experience Manager
**Context**: BO Administration, ISS, SmartEdit
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-9)

## Quote

> "I want our site to be noticed naturally while increasing customer satisfaction and loyalty."

## Overview

Elza's day is filled with tasks to supervise a collection of processes to track and optimize customer interactions. Her main goal is to find efficient ways to manage a customer life cycle, including all the steps customers use to interact with the organization. She aims to foster customer loyalty through quality interactions and personalized content. This improves the experiences for customers, which leads to increased customer satisfaction.

## Key Responsibilities

- Supervise processes to track & optimize experiences
- Foster customer loyalty through high-quality interactions
- Improve the experiences customers have with organizations
- Research customer behavior & increase customer satisfaction
- Personalize content for website & social medias
- Manage personalized content

### SaaS
- AI support for optimization & full utilization of tool capability
