# Diana - Service Agent

**Role**: Service Agent
**Context**: BO Administration, BO Customer Support, ASM
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-6)

## Quote

> "I handle a high volume of calls while providing individual experience for each customer."

## Overview

Diana is at the front line of customer service. She is responsible for ensuring a flawless customer experience by providing timely solutions to customers through various communication channels. This includes addressing customer inquiries about orders, shipment tracking, and product information. Additionally, she focuses on customer retention and may use opportunities to create leads, cross-sell, or up-sell. She collects and analyzes customer feedback to evaluate service quality while identifying areas for improvement.

## Key Responsibilities

- Understand customer inquires & provide resolutions
- Validate customer information & access their schedule
- Provide support for order, shipment, product info, etc
- Improve customer retention
- Deliver an outstanding customer service experience
- Record & maintain customer information
- Search for opportunities to drive sales

### SaaS
- Improve customer satisfaction through timely and effective service resolution
- Streamline the ticket handling process to reduce resolution time
- Enhance knowledge sharing within the service team to improve response accuracy
- Ensure seamless communication across multiple channels

## Challenges

- Database, integration & synchronization of issues
- Lack of documentation & unavailable data
- Difficult data access during calls
- Missing procedures/steps for certain inquiries
- Challenging situation with customers
- Conflicting procedures/policies with customer needs
- Overwhelming amount of inquiries

### SaaS
- Difficulty tailoring SaaS solutions to fit specific service processes
- Reliance on SAP for updates, support, and security
- Potential difficulties in integrating SAP SaaS with non-SAP systems
- Possibly high costs for advanced features or additional user licenses
