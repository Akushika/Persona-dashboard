# Anthony - Content Manager

**Role**: Content Manager
**Context**: BO Administration, ISS, SmartEdit
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-10)

## Quote

> "I am responsible for managing site content and updating product information to support marketing promotions."

## Overview

Anthony maintains website content and applies strategies to increase consumer engagement and conversions. He uses various tools to manage content for multiple regions, and reviews global content changes. During product launches, he collaborates with marketing and category managers to align marketing plan with updated content. In addition, Anthony works closely with them to support search strategies and ensure that the right products are displayed on the site.

## Key Responsibilities

- Research trending topics to create & maintain content
- Apply content strategies to engage consumers & convert
- Support content for multiple regions & their sites
- Keep content up to date: HP, PDP, store finder, navigation, etc
- Adjust, align content for global & local sites
- Manage content for product launches & promotions
- Manage Digital Assets (DAM)
