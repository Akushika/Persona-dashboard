## Overview

Johannes handles specific categories of merchandise and makes decisions about which products to sell. To achieve this he categorizes products and assigns attributes to products (category, price, promotion eligibility). He also, manages product assortment and visual assets of the product categories for an engaging experience on the company website, along with a plan for the site navigation around categories. Part of his job is to maintain product catalogs, update product content, support product pricing and run product positioning strategies.
