# Sarah - Sales Representative Manager

**Role**: Sales Representative Manager
**Context**: ASM, BO Administration, BO Customer Support, Storefront
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-26)

## Quote

> "I want to keep the team inspired while developing and managing compelling co-selling strategies and campaigns."

## Overview

Sarah creates and nurtures high performance sales teams leading them to generate hit revenue forecasts and meet customer needs. Developing and managing compelling co-selling strategies and campaigns is her main goal. One characteristic of her management style is "focus less on selling ability and more on interpersonal skills". She coaches, advises, motivates, and replaces personnel aiming to created a high performance sales force.

## Key Responsibilities

- Keep team inspired & recognize their efforts
- Act as a conductor of the company's revenue engine
- Create and nurture high performance sales teams
- Generate hit revenue forecasts
- Focus on interpersonal skills: coach, advise & motivate team
- Create a high performance sales force
- Meet customer needs & ensure customers is satisfied

## Challenges

- Find qualified lead & maintain customer relationship post-sale
- Ask right questions to prospects and long wait for responses
- Short decision-making window
- Standing apart from competitors
- No single dashboard & spend time on administrative tasks
- Retaining top talent and balance representatives accounts
- Turnover rates near 35% can be expensive
