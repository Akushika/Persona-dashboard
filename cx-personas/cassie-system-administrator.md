# Cassie - System Administrator

**Role**: Customer System Administrator
**Context**: BO Administration
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-1)

## Quote

> "I'm the first user to gain access to Model T."

## Overview

As an administrator, Cassie integrates and connects customer zone to her organizational services. She grants permissions, integrates functional business teams, works on configuration, setup and deployment. She maintains the SAP environment, including systems and databases while ensuring payment gateways and other security-sensitive integration points are configured and working correctly. She will drop performance when things are not working properly prompting her to raise support tickets and follow up on resolutions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Integration of the functional business teams
- Configure payment gateways & other services
- Setup, run and validate the complete landscape
- Maintain the environment & communicate deployments
- Grant permissions & report operational metrics
- Manage connectivity with SAP

## Challenges

- Poor visibility of system interaction / availability
- Unnecessary complexity to make configurations & setups
- Lack of visibility of stakeholders affected by an issue
- Difficulty with integration of different services
- Lack of documentation
