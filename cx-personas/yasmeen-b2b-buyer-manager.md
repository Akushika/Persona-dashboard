# Yasmeen - B2B Buyer Manager

**Role**: B2B Buyer Manager
**Context**: BO Administration, Storefront
**Reference**: [Aha! Persona](https://sap.aha.io/products/COMPANY/personas/COMPANY-P-15)

## Overview

During the merchandise selection process, Yasmeen conducts thorough research on quality, sourcing, timing, delivery, product mix, and promotion. She actively monitors competitor prices and forecasts the top-selling SKUs. Additionally, she keeps a close eye on recurring plan goals to ensure alignment and deliver a compelling value proposition. Yasmeen plays a crucial role in achieving gross margin targets and maintaining a balanced total of inventory.

## Key Responsibilities

- Select & purchase merchandise
- Develop & launch merchandising programs
- Monitor prices with competitors & strategize accordingly
- Forecast top selling SKUs
- Drive sales & improve selling capability
- Identify market opportunities & analyze growth areas
- Drive growth to define volume configurations

### SaaS
- Developing strong, productive relationships with key vendors

## Challenges

- Late delivery
- Data obsolescence due to lack/slow communication
- Request of approval from stakeholders/upper management
- No single dashboard & extra clicks in ordering
- Ordered products do not match quality requirements
- Restricted access offline & on mobile as well

### SaaS
- Managing complex procurement processes and approvals
- Addressing vendor performance issues and contract non-compliance
