## Overview

She is responsible for customizing systems according to specific client needs and reviewing the build output process. She provides support by measuring the application's capacity and performance. She makes test deployments locally and checks in code for all extensions to the customer's GIT repository. She works on both B2C and B2B stores to ensure secure online store development, creates/integrates modules along with add-ons, and customizes shopping carts.
